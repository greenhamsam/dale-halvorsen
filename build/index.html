Dale Halvorsen

Plot consulting

Got a book manuscript, comic, film or TV pitch with a broken plot? I can help you figure out what’s wrong, and suggest some creative ways to fix it.

Some people tame beasts, I help to tame unruly plots.

Dale Halvorsen is the creator and writer of the award-winning Vertigo comic series, Survivors’ Club, which he wrote with Lauren Beukes. It’s now being adapted for TV by The CW. Dale has had two other television show concepts optioned.

He’s worked as a plot consultant for Lauren Beukes, Sam Wilson and Sam Beckbessinger.

Dale has an encyclopaedic knowledge of horror, crime and mystery genres across film, television and fiction, and a knack for fixing plotholes.

Services & Pricing

I will analyse your concept, characters, and plot, and offer you creative suggestions about how to fix the issues. I do not do line-editing and won’t rewrite your manuscript or pitch for you: it’s still your story.

Synopsis analysis: $95

  • Submit a 2-5 page synopsis of your work. I will analyse it for problems.
  • Includes a 1 hour Skype call to discuss my suggestions, and a written summary of my findings.
  • You may also submit a maximum of 2,000 words of your work to demonstrate the style of the piece. You’re welcome to submit additional pages at the rate of $10 per 1,000 words.
  • Turnaround time: 2 weeks.

Synopsis analysis: $95

  • Submit your full manuscript (maximum 90,000 words). I will analyse it for problems.
  • Manuscripts longer than 90,000 words will incur an additional fee of $10 per 1,000 words.
  • Includes 2 hour-long Skype calls to discuss my suggestions, and a written summary of my findings.
  • Turnaround time: 4 weeks.

This isn't a magic wand, I can't guarantee a specific outcome and I can't help you get your story published. What I offer is seasoned subjective advice and a nuanced understanding of plot and character and what makes a story work. The hard work of implementing the fixes is all on you.

The costs above don’t include a second round of review once you have addressed my suggestions. If you’d like me to read your synopsis or manuscript again after you’ve fixed it, that would be treated as a new project.
